MindLink

Overview

MindLink is a psychology-driven concept designed to address loneliness and lack of accessible mental health support among students.

Problem

Students face emotional stress and isolation, while existing solutions are either expensive, inaccessible, or lack relatability.

Core Insight

Mental health solutions must focus on connection, trust, and relatability — not just accessibility.

Solution

• AI-based self-assessment tools
• Community interaction spaces
• Access to therapists
• Productivity and focus tools

Approach

Built using user-centric thinking and behavioral insights, focusing on emotional and functional needs.

Impact Potential

Can improve accessibility and normalize mental health conversations among youth.

Key Learning

Human-centered design is critical when solving problems related to emotions and behavior.
