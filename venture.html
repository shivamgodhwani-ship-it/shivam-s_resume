Metro Card Sleeve Venture

Overview

A micro-venture built with ₹250 capital and executed within a 30-minute selling constraint, focusing on maximizing returns through structured decision-making and rapid execution.

Problem

The challenge was to create and sell a product within extreme constraints of time and capital, requiring quick validation and efficient strategy.

Approach

• Applied SCAMPER framework to innovate product
• Identified target audience (students/metro users)
• Conducted quick validation through observation and feedback
• Used dual-channel strategy (offline + WhatsApp)

Execution

Focused on pricing strategy, demand estimation, and product positioning under time pressure.

Outcome

• ~66% ROI
• ~40% profit margin
• Strong demand validation

Key Learning

Speed, clarity, and structured thinking are critical in real-world business execution.
