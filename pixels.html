Pixels & Profits

Overview

A case competition at IIM Bangalore focused on solving business problems using structured and analytical thinking.

Problem Understanding

The challenge involved identifying underlying gaps in business performance beyond surface-level inefficiencies.

Core Insight

The issue was not purely operational — it was rooted in lack of emotional connection and trust with users.

Solution Strategy

• Reframed problem from user perspective
• Identified behavioral gaps
• Focused on trust-building mechanisms
• Developed human-centric strategy

Outcome

Awarded Best Human Connection for depth and clarity of solution.

Key Learning

Effective business strategies require both structured thinking and deep understanding of human behavior.
